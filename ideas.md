## Idea A — "Street Market Night" (Dark & Moody)
- **Design Movement:** Middle Eastern Night Bazaar / Street Food Noir
- **Core Principles:** Dark warmth, bold contrast, tactile texture, street-food energy
- **Color Philosophy:** Deep charcoal (#1A1A1A) background with warm amber (#C8511B orange) accents and cream (#F5F0E8) text. Evokes the warm glow of a shawarma spit at night.
- **Layout Paradigm:** Full-width hero with the logo centered on a dark textured background. Below, sticky horizontal category nav tabs. Menu items in a masonry-style card grid with hover glow.
- **Signature Elements:** Subtle grain texture overlay, orange glow on hover cards, dashed dividers in warm gold
- **Interaction Philosophy:** Cards lift and glow on hover. Category tabs slide with an underline indicator. Smooth scroll to sections.
- **Animation:** Fade-in-up on scroll for menu cards, tab indicator slides smoothly
- **Typography System:** Oswald (700) for headings + Open Sans (300/400/700) for body

## Idea C — "Bold Fast-Food" (High Contrast & Energetic)
- **Design Movement:** Modern Fast-Food Brand / Bold Editorial
- **Core Principles:** Maximum contrast, large typography, energetic layout, upsell-first hierarchy
- **Color Philosophy:** Pure white background with black section blocks and orange (#C8511B) as the dominant accent. Prices in oversized orange numerals. Feels like a premium fast-food brand.
- **Layout Paradigm:** Full-width alternating dark/light section bands. Combo deals in a full-width dark banner with large price callouts. Category navigation as a horizontal scrollable pill row.
- **Signature Elements:** Oversized price numerals, full-width dark combo banner, diagonal section dividers
- **Interaction Philosophy:** Aggressive hover states — items scale up, prices pop in orange. Combo cards have a "Add to order" CTA.
- **Animation:** Bold slide-in from left on scroll, price numbers count up on first view
- **Typography System:** Bebas Neue (display) + Lato (body)
